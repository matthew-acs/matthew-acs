# Matthew Acs

I am a Computer Science PhD candidate at Florida Atlantic University focused on AI/ML systems, reinforcement learning, healthcare AI, and autonomous systems.

My work spans reinforcement learning, imitation learning, robotic learning, surgical robotics, and medical AI. I am interested in applied AI roles where rigorous model evaluation, implementation feasibility, and clear technical communication matter.

## Research Areas
- Reinforcement learning and offline RL
- Imitation learning and behavior cloning
- Healthcare AI and surgical robotics
- Model evaluation and experimental design
- Autonomous systems
